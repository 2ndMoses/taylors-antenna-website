❓ Common Pricing Questions

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all installations. For complex jobs, we may need to visit your site to provide an accurate quote - this diagnostic visit is $100 but is deducted from your final bill if you proceed with the work.

Are there any hidden costs?

Never! Our pricing is completely transparent. All standard materials, mounting hardware, and installation are included in our quoted prices. Any additional costs will be discussed and approved by you before we proceed.

What's included in the installation price?

Our installation prices include the antenna or equipment, premium mounting hardware, professional installation, cable connections, signal testing, and optimization. We guarantee all our work for years to come.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger jobs, we're happy to discuss payment arrangements. Contact us to discuss your specific needs - we're here to help make quality antenna installation affordable.

What if I need repairs later?

All our work is guaranteed. If you experience any issues with our installation, we'll return to fix it at no charge. We stand behind our work because we take pride in getting it right the first time.
