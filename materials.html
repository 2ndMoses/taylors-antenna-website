Professional Equipment & Components

Premium Connections

All connections are professionally crimped using double bubble waterproof connectors. F-Type connections provide superior screening and maximum reliability in Australian weather conditions.

Australian Made

We exclusively use Australian-made antennas from Hills, Matchmaster, and Digitek - brands specifically designed and tested for Australia's unique climate and broadcasting conditions.

Quality Cabling

Premium RG6 quad-shield coaxial cable with thick center core for maximum signal integrity. Purpose-built for Australian conditions with UV-resistant outer jacket.

Top of the Range Amplifiers

Professional masthead and distribution amplifiers from leading manufacturers. Variable gain control, low noise figure, and excellent overload characteristics for optimal signal boosting in all conditions.

Filters, Attenuators & Splitters

Premium filtering solutions including 4G/5G filters, band-pass filters, and variable attenuators. High-quality splitters and combiners with excellent isolation and low insertion loss for multi-TV installations.

Professional Antenna Selection

Every installation begins with professional site assessment and signal testing. We choose the optimal antenna based on your location, signal strength, terrain, and local conditions - ensuring perfect reception with the right antenna for your specific needs.

Our Premium Antenna Selection

We carefully select from Australia's leading antenna manufacturers - Hills, Matchmaster, and Digitek. These brands represent the pinnacle of Australian antenna engineering, designed specifically for our unique climate and broadcasting environment.

Hills DY4 VHF Antenna

Hills DY6 VHF Antenna

The DY6 is ideal for areas just outside capital cities. Features wider bandwidth for digital reception, F-Type connection, and heavy-duty powder-coated construction designed for Australian weather.

Hills DY10 VHF Antenna

High-gain antenna for semi-rural areas requiring extra signal strength. Features superior F-Type connection and robust aluminium construction. Perfect for challenging VHF reception areas.

The latest evolution in UHF antennas, featuring BoomLock™ technology, ultrasonically sealed balun housing, and PCB balun for true impedance matching. Designed and manufactured in Australia with lifetime warranty.

About Our Chosen Brands

Hills Antennas

Australia's most trusted antenna brand with over 70 years of experience. Hills antennas feature their patented BoomLock™ technology and are designed specifically for Australian digital television. All Hills antennas come with a lifetime warranty and are built to withstand our harsh climate.

Perfect For: Coastal homes in Murdunna, Eaglehawk Neck, and other exposed locations along the Tasman Peninsula where wind and salt spray are constant challenges.

Matchmaster

Premium German-engineered antennas, assembled in Australia. Matchmaster specializes in phased array technology with superior gain and interference rejection. Their antennas feature IP66 rated baluns and are perfect for coastal and challenging reception areas.

Australian-owned company manufacturing heavy-duty digital antennas with 12mm extruded aluminium elements. Digitek antennas feature UV-stabilized plastics, stainless steel hardware, and bifurcated rivets - the strongest in the industry. All built for Australian conditions.

Best For: Rural properties in Copping, Carlton, and other areas where you need maximum signal gain and durability against Tasmania's variable weather conditions.
